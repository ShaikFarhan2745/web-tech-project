About AMIGLORIA

The biggest cultural extravaganza of Amity University Bengaluru, bringing together talent, creativity, and innovation in one spectacular event.

Celebrate Excellence

AMIGLORIA 2025 is more than just a festival – it's a celebration of diverse talents, creative expression, and cultural unity. Join us for three days of unforgettable performances, competitions, and experiences.

With over 50+ events spanning music, dance, drama, literary arts, and technical competitions, there's something for everyone to showcase their skills and passion.
